Our mission

Open and affordable

Our cheat sheet is providing cost-effective solutions for students. We value affordability to ensure that all students, regardless of financial conditions, can access valuable food resources around the campus to enhance their academic experiences.

Diversity

Diversity is the core mission of our cheat sheet which can provide students with diverse choices of food choices. We appreciate and celebrate the unique backgrounds and different needs of students. Our website is here to cater various students' learning styles and preferences.

Time efficiency

Time is the key. Cheap Eats Sheet provides quick and easy restaurant options around the campus which can save time when students have heavy study workloads. Our website is designed to help students balance both academic and personal responsibilities without compromising on healthy and affordable meal options.

About us
